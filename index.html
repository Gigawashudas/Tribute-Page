Dr. Joy Gopal Das

Here's a time line of Dr. Joy Gopal Das's:

  • 1959 - Born in Shariatpur, Dhaka
  • 1979 - Leaves his family to attend the Sher-E-Bangla Medical College.
  • 1987 - Finishes medical college and takes a job at CHCP.
  • 1990 - Marries Smriti Kana.
  • 1993 - his wife give birth to his only child.
  • 1997 - Netherlands visit.
  • 2008 - His wife dies and he lost his job.
  • 2010 - Starts rebuild everything. Get the job back. CCD.
  • 2013 - Moves to Agailjhara.
  • 2015 - Join Patuakhali Diabetic Society (Birdem Hospital, Patuakhali).
  • 2021 - Promoted to director.
